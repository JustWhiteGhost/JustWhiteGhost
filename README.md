## What I Do

I build the parts of game worlds that don't get the spotlight — terrain systems, foliage, atmosphere, the stuff that makes a scene feel *alive* rather than just look nice. On the side I model low-poly and mid-poly props, weapons, and environments, and texture them by hand or in Substance Painter.

**Game Development** — Programming since I was young across C++, C#, Python, Java, JavaScript, GDScript, and HTML. Unity is my main engine. After the 2023 pricing situation I explored Godot and genuinely think it's solid for 2D *(3D is workable but I'll take Unity or Unreal for that)*. Environmental systems and tooling are where I spend most of my time.

**3D Art** — Environmental art, props, weapons, artifacts, and shaders. I can hand-paint textures or use Substance Painter depending on the style. Characters and 2D illustration are works in progress — not there yet, won't pretend otherwise.

**Story / Boarding** — Genuinely struggling with this one. If you have tips, I'm listening. No, seriously.
